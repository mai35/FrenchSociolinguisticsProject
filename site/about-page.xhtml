The Académie

France is known for being very protective of its language, and even has the Académie française, whose mission it is to correct unfavorable speech and keep the French language prestigious. Lately this involves discouraging invasive loanwords, many of which come from English. We wanted to focus on these Anglicisms, and look at the Académie's actual influence over French.
